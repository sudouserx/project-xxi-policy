Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Introduction

Project XXI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

The core philosophy of Project XXI is privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers.

Data Storage

All data generated within Project XXI, including your habits, progress, logs, and preferences, is stored locally on your device using SQLite database technology.

We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back it up yourself.

Data Collection

Since the app operates entirely offline and locally:

  • We do not collect personal identification information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use third-party analytics services that track individual user behavior.

Permissions

Project XXI requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Notifications: Used solely to send you local reminders for your habits and challenges. These are scheduled locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any user data at all, this applies to all users regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
